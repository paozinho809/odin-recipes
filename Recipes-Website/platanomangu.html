Platano Mangu

What is it?

This dish is so iconic of the Dominican Republic. This can be had at any time of the day, but it is most commonly had for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 Green plantains
  • Salt
  • 1 cup of reserved water from the pot
  • 4 tablespoons of Butter

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains and cut into pieces about 1/2" thick
  2. Boil in Salted Water for 20 minutes. Make sure a fork can easily pierce the piecs
  3. As plantains finish cooking, reserve 1 cup of the water from the pot then draink the rest. Pour plantain pieces into a large bowl over the butter
  4. Begin mashing, add the 1 cup water in increments.
  5. Mash to the consistency desired
  6. let sit for a few minutes before serving, season with salt
  7. top with picked red onions
